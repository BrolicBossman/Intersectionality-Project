Intersectionality

Intersectionality is a concept that breaks down different social identities like gender, race, ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation, disability, class and other forms of identity to link them with systematic inequalities to explain the outcomes and affects of discrimination agaisnt those groups

In The urgency of intersectionality, a TedTalk that was done by Kimberlé Crenshaw. She states "The law, the law is like that, that ambulance that shows up and is ready to treat Emma only if it can be shown that she was harmed on the race row or the gender road but not where those roads interesected"
